Our Spiritual Guides

Meet the visionaries behind our transformative journey

Rishi Kumar

With over five years dedicated to spiritual awakening through Eastern traditions, Rishi Kumar brings profound wisdom to our sanctuary. After studying ancient yogic practices in the Himalayas, he developed his unique approach to inner transformation that combines meditation, breathwork, and energy healing.

As our lead meditation guide, Rishi's gentle yet powerful presence helps seekers release deep-seated emotional blocks and reconnect with their true essence. His background in quantum physics gives him a rare ability to bridge spiritual concepts with scientific understanding.

"The universe speaks in silence. When we quiet our minds, we begin to hear its guidance."

Jeevietha

Jeevietha's journey began in the ancient temples of Tamil Nadu, where she studied the sacred art of sound healing and mantras. Her soulful voice carries vibrations that penetrate the deepest layers of consciousness, facilitating profound healing and spiritual alignment.

As our ceremonial guide, Jeevietha creates sacred spaces that honor both ancient traditions and modern spiritual seeking. Her background in Carnatic music and Vedic chanting brings authenticity to our sound healing sessions, while her intuitive abilities help participants connect with their higher selves.

"Sound is the original creative force. When we align our vibration with universal harmony, transformation becomes inevitable."

Satvika

Satvika discovered her spiritual path through the transformative power of Ayurveda and yogic nutrition. After healing herself from chronic illness through plant medicine and energy work, she dedicated her life to helping others achieve holistic wellness that encompasses body, mind, and spirit.

As our wellness curator, Satvika designs our nourishing food experiences and cleansing protocols. Her knowledge of herbs, crystals, and subtle energy systems creates a foundation for deep physical purification that supports spiritual growth. Her approach emphasizes the sacred connection between nature and human consciousness.

"The body is your temple, the mind your altar. Nourish both with intention, and divinity will reveal itself."

Saanvi

Saanvi's spiritual journey began in childhood with vivid dreams that connected her to ancestral wisdom. After formal training in Jungian psychology and shamanic practices, she developed a unique approach to dream interpretation and subconscious exploration that bridges Eastern spirituality with Western understanding.

As our intuitive guide, Saanvi leads transformative workshops on accessing inner wisdom and developing psychic abilities. Her gentle approach makes mystical practices accessible to beginners while offering depth for experienced practitioners. Her background in sacred geometry informs our space design and energy alignment practices.

"Dreams are the soul's whispers. When we learn their language, we discover maps to treasures hidden within."
